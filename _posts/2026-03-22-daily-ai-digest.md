* **tiny corp** is now funded and hiring full-time software engineers and talented interns who contribute to tinygrad, with positions also available in operations and hardware
* **tinybox** is a high-performance deep learning computer now shipping in three versions: red v2 (778 TFLOPS, $12,000), green v2 blackwell (3086 TFLOPS, $65,000), and the upcoming exabox (~1 EXAFLOP, ~$10M, coming 2027)
* The framework supports full forward and backward passes with autodiff, is used in openpilot for autonomous driving, and aims to be 2x faster than PyTorch through custom kernel compilation, lazy tensor evaluation, and aggressive operation fusion
* tinygrad is currently in alpha and development happens on GitHub and Discord, with the goal to commoditize the petaflop and enable AI for everyone

  * Built-in OCR (80+ languages), LaTeX formula extraction, AI-generated image descriptions

  * Built in collaboration with PDF Association and veraPDF developers

* **Why it's notable**: Ranks #1 in independent benchmarks across 200 real-world PDFs, beating established tools like Docling and Marker. The accessibility auto-tagging feature is the first open-source solution to generate Tagged PDFs end-to-end, validated against Well-Tagged PDF specifications.

* **功能介绍**: ClawTeam让AI智能体能够自组织成协作集群,自主处理复杂任务。用户只需提供高层目标,系统会自动生成专业化子智能体、分配任务、管理依赖关系,并通过智能协调交付结果,无需手动协调多个智能体。

* **主要特点**:
  * 自动生成具有隔离环境的智能体(git工作树、tmux窗口)
  * 通过CLI命令实现智能体间实时通信
  * 兼容Claude Code、Codex、OpenClaw、nanobot、Cursor等任何CLI智能体
  * 内置应用场景:AI研究自动化(机器学习实验)、智能体工程(全栈开发)、AI对冲基金(市场分析与交易)
  * 简单基础设施 - 仅需文件系统和tmux,无需复杂消息队列或容器
  * 基于TOML的模板系统,支持自定义集群配置
  * 提供Web界面和tmux面板监控集群活动

* **为何值得关注**: ClawTeam代表了从孤立AI智能体到集群智能的范式转变。该项目获得2438星标,展示了大规模实用自动化能力 - 例如在8个H100 GPU上优化LLM训练,自主完成2400+实验并实现6.4%性能提升,全程无需人工干预。该框架专为智能体通过CLI自主使用而设计,使其在真正自主操作方面独树一帜,而非依赖人工编写的编排代码。
